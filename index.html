ITSO's impactful programs and community service headed by Harvy Virocel, ITSO President and Mr. Ariel Dela Cruz, Program Head of IT department have set a new standard for success and have significantly contributed to the betterment of our institution.

2nd ISITE National Conforence

To foster camaraderie from among IT enthusiasts from different schools, colleges, and universities in the Philippines and to provide Information Technology updates, the 2nd iSITE National Conference with the theme “Innovate 360: Navigating the Digital Frontier in Education” was conducted on April 5, 2024 at the University of Cordillera (UC) Main Campus, Baguio City. The delegation from ISPSC Candon Campus headed by Mr. Ariel Dela Cruz, BSIT Program Head, attended the said conference. There were nineteen Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students who participated actively in the discussions and various activities. Ms. Clariza Valdez, BSIT Faculty, also attended the conference. The event is an annual activity organized by the Integrated Society of Information Technology Enthusiasts (iSITE) Inc., spearheaded by its president, Dr. Neil P. Balba.
